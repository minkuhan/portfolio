ZetoTec

Client. ZetoTec
Year. 2018
Range of Work. UX Design & Graphic Design
Software. Adobe Illustrator, Figma, InVision

Description & My Role.
ZetoTec is a start-up company based in Waterloo providing a mobile application that gives small business all the support functions of a back-office on their smartphone including employee tracking, In-app consulting, and many more features.
I worked as a Visual Designer & Digital Marketing Specialist and designed prototypes for the mobile application as well as front-end website. I also designed the promotional materials, including PowerPoint Slides and flyers, to promote and the product as well as to increase brand awareness.
